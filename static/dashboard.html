How Time Works

The game runs for 10 years in 6-month steps.
Each step the economy shifts — rates move, prices change, rents rise or fall.
You make one decision per step: buy, sell, upgrade, or hold.
Twenty decisions. Ten years. One outcome.

The Hidden Era

Interest rates, house prices, inflation and rent growth are drawn from real UK history.
The start year is hidden. You might be in the dot-com era, the 2008 crash, the post-COVID rate shock, or the long low-rate recovery.
Read the market. Adapt.

Risks to Watch

DEBT SERVICEABILITY (ICR)
Rental income must cover mortgage interest by at least 1.25× at rates +2%. Shortfalls are costed against your final score.
HIGH LTV
Borrowing above 60% LTV adds a capital risk cost — your estimated loss in a 20% price correction scales with leverage.
EPC MANDATE
At game start, F and G rated properties are void — no rent until upgraded. A government mandate arrives mid-game requiring EPC C or better. Non-compliant properties go void after a 2-year grace period, and each reduces your final score.
CONCENTRATION
More than 60% of your portfolio in one region adds a regional downturn cost to your final score.

Your Actions

UPG — Upgrade
Improve a property's EPC rating by 2 bands. Rent rises 10%, value rises. Removes void risk from non-compliant properties.
REFI — Refinance
Borrow against your equity at 75% LTV. Releases cash to buy more properties. Only available on variable-rate mortgages.
RENO — Renovate
Spend 10% of property value to raise rent 15% and value 8%. One-time only per property. Does not change EPC rating.

Your Analytics Panel

Properties
How many properties you currently own.
Voids
Properties currently generating no rent — either empty or EPC non-compliant. Shown in red if any.
Gross Yield
Annual rent as a % of portfolio value. Higher is better income.
Capital Growth
How much your properties have grown in value since you bought them. Shown in brackets if negative.
ROE
Annual net income (rent minus mortgage interest) as a % of the equity you hold. Low ROE means idle equity — consider refinancing to redeploy it.
CoC Return
Cash-on-cash — net income vs cash you've deployed. Shows how hard your money is working.
Refi Headrm
How much equity you could release by refinancing at 75% LTV.
EPC Risk
Value of properties below the current EPC standard. Pre-mandate: F and G are non-compliant. Post-mandate: D–G. Each non-compliant property carries a 15% scoring haircut on its value — upgrade to remove the penalty.
Cash
Money available to spend on deposits, upgrades, or emergencies.
How to win: Your final score is total return minus risk cost. Risk cost is the sum of four real deductions: ICR stress shortfall, LTV capital exposure, regional concentration, and EPC non-compliance. A high-leverage strategy might win big — or face a crippling ICR penalty if rates rise. The best investor adapts. The worst one sticks to a plan the market has already broken.
